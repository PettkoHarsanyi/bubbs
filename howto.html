The game is somewhat like a "Battle Royale" game, You have to reach the green safe zone with the blue ball before the gray zone runs in.
There are obstacles as well, if You touch a red ball, You lose.
You can move with "WASD" and sprint with Left Shift.

If You get to the green safe zone in time, the round is over, and You get +10 points.
You start the next round from the position You were, but the green zone will be somewhere else.
The task is the same, but watch out, there are more red zones this time.

There is also a gold zone that also runs into the green zone, but if You get there before that, You get bonus combo points.

You can also stack combos if You make it into the gold zone multiple times in a row. This way, You can score a lot of points. For example, if You combo 5 times, You will get 50 points by multiplying the original 10 points by 5.

If You crash into a red circle or don't make it to the green zone in time during the crazy rush pressing the Left Shift, unfortunately the game is over for You, the cat is left outside.
